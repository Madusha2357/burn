The new SeaXplorer 80 is the ultimate expedition yacht. With 40 days of full luxury autonomy and compliance with IMO-polar codes, this vessel has all the capabilities needed for real expeditions.

Enjoy a fantastic party on the aft deck with up to 150 guests, carry all your mega-tenders up to 11,8m and operate your aircraft all season from your SeaXplorers self- sufficient floating airfield.

SeaXplorer Range and Speed

The SeaXplorer range is designed to make unique expedition experiences possible. With cutting-edge technology, the SeaXplorer 80 offers impressive speed and range capabilities to ensure you can explore the most remote corners of the world with ease.
