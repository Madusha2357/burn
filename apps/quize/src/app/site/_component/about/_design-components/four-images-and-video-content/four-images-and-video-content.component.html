EYOS expeditions

Since pioneering the category in 2008, EYOS remains the world leader in private superyacht expeditions. This expertise is grounded in hard-won experience, decades of expeditions to the world’s wildest places, where knowledge, self-sufficiency and safety standards are the only assurances.

EYOS’ full-time shoreside team has over 350 years of collective expedition experience and represents a network of travel and marine specialists that spans seven continents.

Specialising in bespoke itineraries, permitting, complex logistics, marine consulting and more, EYOS enables yacht owners, captains, and charterers to dream bigger and confidently set their course for the most remote and culturally rich parts of the world.
